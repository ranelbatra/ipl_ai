# Sprint Plan – IPL Intelligence Studio

---

## Sprint 1 – Team DNA

### Goal
Develop an AI-powered Team DNA module that identifies the unique playing identity and strategic behavior of every IPL franchise.

### Tasks
- Design Team DNA analytics
- Analyze batting and bowling tendencies
- Generate AI-based team personality
- Create Team DNA dashboard
- Validate insights using historical matches

### Deliverables
- Team DNA module
- Team Personality Report
- Team DNA visualization

---

## Sprint 2 – AI Documentary

### Goal
Generate AI-powered documentary style narratives that tell the complete story of an IPL match.

### Tasks
- Design storytelling prompts
- Generate match narratives
- Highlight turning points
- Present documentary inside Streamlit
- Improve readability and formatting

### Deliverables
- AI Documentary Generator
- Interactive documentary page
- Match storytelling interface

---

## Sprint 3 – Match Aura

### Goal
Develop Match Aura, an AI-powered feature that evaluates the emotional intensity and atmosphere of every IPL match.

### Tasks
- Design Aura scoring algorithm
- Generate Match Aura summaries
- Calculate excitement level
- Visualize Aura indicators
- Integrate with Streamlit dashboard

### Deliverables
- Match Aura Engine
- Aura Dashboard
- AI-generated match atmosphere report
